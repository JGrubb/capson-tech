The Industry's Most Powerful Technology

Designed to streamline and automate distribution, underwriting, and acquisition processes for medical malpractice insurance, QuickClick is an industry first, incorporating a Prospect Portal and Sales Portal, both of which work together seamlessly to allow for the most efficient and user-friendly acquisition and underwriting engine of its kind.

Beyond Technology

Our technology goes beyond simply streamlining the onboarding of new physicians, it dramatically improves the consistency and thoroughness of underwriting overall. By integrating relevant and specific underwriting data from the industry's most reliable sources into the algorithm, we're able to significantly expedite the underwriting process and improve underwriting decisions.

The Industry's Most Powerful Products

Prospect Portal

The process of enrolling for medical malpractice insurance coverage is now much simpler. Designed for ease of use by solo physicians, office managers, and group practice administrators, Prospect Portal features realtime pricing, enabling providers to see price indications that reflect the latest information that has been entered.

Sales Portal

A companion to Prospect Portal, targeted at insurance carriers and third-party agents. It gives agents up to the minute enrollment status on their prospects and facilitates the new business workflow between providers, agents, and the carrier.
